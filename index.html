About

I hold a PhD in computational biology and spent 8+ years in academic research — cancer genomics, developmental biology, and bioinformatics tools published in top journals.

I build pipelines that make large-scale biological data interpretable: precision oncology, mitochondrial dynamics, synthetic plasmid scoring — running on private HPCs and cloud infrastructures (AWS).

Lead bioinformatician and scientific advisor at Allovista (US) and SeCore (UK), translating research-grade methods into production-ready platforms.
